Maung Thant

I am currently conducting a full-time research assistantship with Prof. Vincenzo Lomonaco on continual model selection for tool learning in continual learning systems.

I am a recent graduate of the Advanced Master's (Master-after-Master) in Artificial Intelligence at KU Leuven, where I worked with Prof. Bart De Moor on self-supervised multi-modal medical AI systems.

I also hold a Master's in Electronics and ICT Engineering Technology from KU Leuven, where I conducted research with Prof. Jef Peeters on vision-based robotic grasping, Prof. Toon van Waterschoot on automatic polyphonic music transcription through spatial audio recording, and Prof. Lucca Geurts on health monitor system for detecting and predicting asthma attacks.

My research interests span deep learning, unsupervised representation learning, multi-modality, continual learning, dynamic scene understanding, and healthcare AI.
